How SewBridge Works

For Customers

1

Browse Tailors

Explore our verified tailors. Filter by specialty, location, and ratings to find your perfect match.

2

Request an Order

Click "Request Order" on a tailor's profile, describe your garment, and book securely online.

3

Discuss Details

Message with your tailor about measurements, style preferences, fabric, and timeline.

4

Receive & Review

Get your custom garment, track fittings via secure messaging, and review your tailor.

For Tailors

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and build your tailor profile. Showcase your specialties, pricing, and previous work.

2

Get Verified

Complete our verification process to earn a trusted badge and appear in search results.

3

Receive Orders

Accept orders, communicate with customers, and manage measurements all in one dashboard.

4

Grow Your Business

Build your reputation, increase earnings, and grow your customer base month over month.
